Pizza Bagel

Description

Quick and easy 10 minute homemade bagel recipe. If you are in a rush for something quick and delicious, this is for you! All you need are bagels, pasta sauce of your choice, and toppings of your choice. I wil be showing you how to make my favorite kind, pepperoni pizza, bagel style! My favorite way is to buy a log of pepperoni and cut thick slices so the pepperoni curls up.

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Pasta Sauce of your choice
  • Low moisture mozzarella
  • Pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. Cut the bagel in half
  2. Spread 2-3 tablespoons of your sauce on each side of the bagel
  3. Put as little or many toppings as you would like, I will use thick cut pepperoni
  4. Add a third, to half cup of cheese, or add more if that is what you like
  5. Put the bagel halves into your air fryer for 7 to 10 minutes on the highest temperature
  6. Leave the bagel in there until your cheese is melted to your liking
