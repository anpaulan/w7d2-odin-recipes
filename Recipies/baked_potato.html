Baked Potato

Description

This is a simple, yet delicious baked potato recipie. Can add more flavor, or just set and forget it

Ingredients

  • Russet Potato
  • Salted Butter

Steps

  1. Cut the potato in half
  2. Preheat the oven/air fryer to 360/375 degrees
  3. Bake for 25-35 minutes until the potatoe is fork tender
  4. Spread butter and toppings of your choice
  5. Optionally, make garlic butter by melting 4 tablespoons of butter and 1 clove of minced garlic
